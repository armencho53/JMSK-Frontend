# Product Overview

Multi-tenant SaaS application for jewelry manufacturers to track supplies, orders, manufacturing workflows, and shipments.

## Core Features

- Multi-tenant architecture with isolated data per manufacturer
- Supply/inventory management
- Customer and company management
- Order tracking with timeline visualization
- Manufacturing workflow tracking (step-by-step process)
- Shipment tracking
- Department balance tracking
- Role-based access control (RBAC)
- JWT authentication with refresh tokens

## Business Domain

Jewelry manufacturing involves:
- Raw materials (supplies) with metal types (gold 24k/22k/18k/14k, silver 925, platinum)
- Weight tracking throughout manufacturing (initial weight, target weight, weight loss per step)
- Multi-step manufacturing processes (casting, polishing, stone setting, etc.)
- Department-based workflow (each step assigned to a department)
- Customer orders with specifications and due dates
- Shipment tracking for completed orders
